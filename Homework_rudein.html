Project Summary

During her reign of 70 years, Queen Elizabeth II managed to give 69 Christmas speeches, also known as Christmas greetings or broadcasts. For my project, I will collect her Christmas greetings from 1952 to 2021 from the royal.uk website, and use Python to study the tetragrams including the lemma "Christmas" as this word probably appears in many of the greetings.

Research questions

  • What are the promiment tetragrams including the lemma "christmas" in Queen Elizabeth II's Christmas Broadcasts?
  • Do the most popular tetragrams appear consistently across the different decades
